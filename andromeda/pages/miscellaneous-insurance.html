Coverage Options

Specialized Insurance Solutions

Our miscellaneous insurance covers niche, specialized, and unique risks across personal and commercial lines that require bespoke coverage solutions.

Personal

Personal Accident Insurance

Provides financial compensation in the event of accidental death, permanent disability, or temporary disability. Pays cash benefits regardless of other insurance policies.

  • Accidental death benefit
  • Permanent total disability
  • Temporary disability income
  • Medical expense reimbursement
  • 24/7 global coverage
Financial

Fidelity Guarantee

Protects employers against financial losses caused by fraudulent or dishonest acts of their employees. Essential for businesses handling cash, securities, or valuable assets.

  • Employee fraud & theft
  • Named or blanket basis
  • Cash & securities cover
  • Computer fraud extension
  • Discovery period cover
Events

Event Insurance

Comprehensive coverage for concerts, conferences, weddings, sports events, exhibitions, and other organized events against cancellation, liability, and property damage.

  • Event cancellation cover
  • Public & venue liability
  • Equipment & props damage
  • Non-appearance of performers
  • Adverse weather cancellation
Legal

Legal Expenses Insurance

Covers the cost of legal proceedings including legal fees, court costs, and expert witness fees for employment disputes, contract disputes, property matters, and more.

  • Employment tribunal cover
  • Contract dispute cover
  • Property rights disputes
  • Tax investigation defense
  • Legal helpline access
High Value

Valuable Goods & Fine Art

Specialized cover for jewelry, fine art, antiques, collectibles, musical instruments, and other high-value items against theft, accidental damage, and mysterious disappearance.

  • Worldwide all-risks cover
  • Theft & mysterious loss
  • In-transit & exhibition cover
  • Professional valuation service
  • Agreed value settlement
Surety

Bonds & Guarantees

Surety bonds providing financial guarantees to fulfill contractual obligations. Includes performance bonds, bid bonds, advance payment bonds, and customs bonds.

  • Performance bonds
  • Bid / tender bonds
  • Advance payment guarantee
  • Customs & excise bonds
  • Retention money bonds
