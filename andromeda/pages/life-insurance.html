Term Life Insurance

Provides coverage for a specific period (10, 20, or 30 years). If you pass away during the term, your beneficiaries receive the death benefit. Affordable and straightforward.

  • Fixed premium for the entire term
  • Death benefit paid tax-free
  • Convertible to permanent coverage

Lifetime Cover

Whole Life Insurance

Permanent coverage that lasts your entire lifetime. Builds cash value over time that you can borrow against or withdraw, making it a financial asset as well as protection.

  • Lifelong guaranteed protection
  • Accumulates cash value
  • Fixed premiums that never increase
  • Policy loans available
  • Dividends may be earned
Flexible

Universal Life Insurance

Flexible permanent coverage with adjustable premiums and death benefits. Earn interest on cash value based on market rates, with the flexibility to adapt as your life changes.

  • Adjustable premiums and benefits
  • Cash value earns interest
  • Withdraw or borrow from cash value
  • Multiple investment sub-accounts
  • Ideal for high-net-worth individuals
Investment-Linked

Investment-Linked Life

Combines life coverage with an investment component. Part of your premium is invested in funds of your choice, offering potential for higher returns alongside protection.

  • Life cover + investment growth
  • Choose from multiple fund options
  • Potential for wealth accumulation
  • Flexible premium allocation
  • Regular fund performance reports
Critical Illness

Critical Illness Cover

Pays a lump sum upon diagnosis of specified critical illnesses such as cancer, heart attack, or stroke. Covers medical costs and income replacement during recovery.

  • Covers 30+ critical conditions
  • Lump sum payment on diagnosis
  • Use payout for any purpose
  • Can be added as a rider
  • No restrictions on use of funds
Group Plan

Group Life Insurance

Cost-effective life coverage for employees or association members. Employers can offer this as a benefit to attract and retain talent while protecting their workforce.

  • Lower premiums per person
  • No individual medical exams
  • Easy enrollment for employees
  • Customizable coverage amounts
  • Scalable for any business size
