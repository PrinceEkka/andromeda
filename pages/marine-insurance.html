Safeguarding trade on the water. Our marine insurance protects your cargo, vessels, and maritime operations against the perils of the sea and beyond.

Coverage Options

Choose Your Marine Insurance Type

From importers and exporters to ship owners and port operators, we provide comprehensive marine risk solutions.

Cargo

Marine Cargo Insurance

Protects goods in transit by sea, air, rail, or road against loss or damage. Covers from the seller's warehouse to the buyer's warehouse — door to door.

  • All-risk or named perils
  • Sea, air, road & rail transit
  • Door-to-door coverage
  • General average contribution
  • War & strikes extension
Hull

Marine Hull Insurance

Covers the physical vessel itself — its hull, machinery, and equipment — against damage from collisions, grounding, fire, and other maritime perils.

  • Hull & machinery damage
  • Collision liability (3/4)
  • Salvage & wreck removal
  • Fire & explosion
  • Piracy cover available
Most Popular

Open Cover Policy

A continuous marine cargo policy ideal for regular importers/exporters. Automatically covers all shipments without issuing individual certificates for each consignment.

  • Continuous automatic cover
  • Monthly declaration basis
  • No per-shipment paperwork
  • Volume premium discounts
  • All commodities option
Liability

Marine Liability (P&I)

Protection & Indemnity cover for ship owners and operators covering third-party liabilities including crew injury, passenger claims, cargo damage, and pollution.

  • Crew injury & death
  • Passenger liability
  • Cargo legal liability
  • Pollution & wreck removal
  • Collision liability (1/4)
Inland

Inland Marine Cover

Covers goods transported on inland waterways, lakes, and rivers as well as mobile equipment, contractors' equipment, and goods in transit on land.

  • Lake & river transit
  • Floating property cover
  • Inland transit all-risks
  • Construction equipment
  • Fine arts & valuables
Fishing Vessels

Fishing Vessel Insurance

Tailored coverage for commercial and artisanal fishing vessels, protecting the boat, fishing equipment, crew, and catch against maritime perils and accidents.

  • Vessel hull & machinery
  • Fishing gear & nets
  • Crew personal accident
  • Catch loss cover
  • Liability to third parties
