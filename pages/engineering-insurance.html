Engineering
Insurance

Protecting the projects that build the future. Our engineering insurance safeguards construction sites, machinery, electronic equipment, and infrastructure projects.

Coverage Options

Choose Your Engineering Insurance Type

From groundbreaking to commissioning, our engineering insurance covers every phase of your project and every type of industrial machinery.

Construction

Contractor's All Risk (CAR)

Comprehensive coverage for civil engineering construction projects — buildings, roads, bridges, dams — from commencement through to project handover.

  • Works under construction
  • Materials on-site & in transit
  • Contractor's plant & equipment
  • Third-party liability
  • Defects after completion
Erection

Erection All Risk (EAR)

Designed for erection and installation of plant, machinery, steel structures, and mechanical equipment. Covers from unloading to testing and commissioning.

  • Machinery during erection
  • Testing & commissioning
  • Transit to site coverage
  • Third-party liability
  • Advanced loss of profit option
Most Popular

Machinery Breakdown

Protects industrial machinery and equipment against sudden, accidental mechanical or electrical breakdown that causes physical damage. Covers repair or replacement costs.

  • Mechanical & electrical failure
  • Operator error coverage
  • Short circuit & overvoltage
  • Consequential loss option
  • Express freight for parts
Electronic

Electronic Equipment Insurance

Specialized coverage for computers, servers, telecom systems, medical devices, and other sensitive electronic equipment against breakdown, theft, and power surge.

  • Hardware damage & theft
  • Power surge & surge
  • External data media
  • Increased working costs
  • Software reinstatement
Boilers

Boiler & Pressure Vessel

Mandatory coverage for businesses operating boilers, pressure vessels, and steam systems. Covers explosion, collapse, and resulting property damage and liability.

  • Explosion & implosion
  • Collapse of vessel
  • Consequential damage
  • Third-party liability
  • Inspection certificate service
Infrastructure

Deterioration of Stock

Covers loss or damage to perishable goods in cold storage or temperature-controlled environments resulting from breakdown of refrigeration or cooling systems.

  • Refrigeration breakdown
  • Power failure cover
  • Spoilage of perishables
  • Contamination losses
  • Business interruption option
