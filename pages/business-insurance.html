Protects company directors and officers from personal financial losses due to wrongful acts, mismanagement claims, regulatory investigations, and shareholder lawsuits.

Product Liability

Covers claims arising from injury or damage caused by products your business manufactures, distributes, or sells. Essential for manufacturers and retailers.

  • Bodily injury from products
  • Property damage claims
  • Product recall expenses
  • Legal & court costs
  • Worldwide product cover
Digital

Cyber Liability

Protects businesses against data breaches, ransomware attacks, and other cyber threats. Covers notification costs, regulatory fines, and business interruption from cyber events.

  • Data breach response
  • Ransomware & extortion
  • Regulatory fines & penalties
  • Business interruption
  • Cyber forensics included
